Synopsis

"John Wick" is a 2014 action thriller film that follows the story of retired hitman John Wick, played by Keanu Reeves, who seeks revenge against the Russian mobsters who killed his beloved dog and stole his car. Wick, who was known as the "Boogeyman" among the criminal underworld, navigates through the seedy underworld of New York City, fighting off hordes of hired killers in his quest for justice. The film is known for its intense and stylish action sequences, including martial arts and gunfights, as well as the memorable performance by Reeves as the determined and deadly John Wick.
